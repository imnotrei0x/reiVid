about

reiVid is a free website that allows you to trim and crop videos directly in your browser.

all processing happens locally on your device.

no data collection, no tracking, no cookies, no ads, no login - but why?
because I had a hard time finding a simple tool.

powered by FFmpeg.wasm, a WebAssembly build of FFmpeg

if you like it, feel free to donate

visit the project on github or on x/twitter
