privacy policy

overview

reiVid by reiLoaded is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

data collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or video content:

  • All video processing occurs entirely in your browser
  • Your videos never leave your device
  • We don't use cookies or tracking
  • We don't collect analytics or usage data

how it works

  • Videos are processed locally using FFmpeg.wasm
  • All computations happen in your browser
  • No data is sent to any servers
  • Device memory and capabilities are checked locally
  • Processed videos are downloaded directly to your device

third-party services

  • We use Vercel for hosting static files
  • FFmpeg.wasm is loaded from CDN
  • No third-party analytics or tracking

your rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is:

  • No personal data to access or delete
  • No data sharing or selling
  • No data retention policies needed
