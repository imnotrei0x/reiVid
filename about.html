about reiVid

reiVid is a free, open-source web application that allows you to trim and crop videos directly in your browser.
all processing happens locally on your device.

powered by FFmpeg.wasm, a WebAssembly build of FFmpeg, it's a simple and efficient way to edit your videos without compromising privacy or quality.


no data collection, no tracking, no cookies, no ads, no login - but why?

because i had a hard time find a simple solution.
